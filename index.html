MEET THE FOUNDER

BRAHMAIAH

Founder & Head Coach · IronForge

Brahmaiah spent 8 years competing on the national powerlifting circuit before founding IronForge in 2010 with a single squat rack and a vision. A certified NSCA Strength & Conditioning Specialist and two-time National Champion, he believed that transformational fitness should be accessible — not a luxury. Today, IronForge stands as the most respected independent gym in the region, built entirely on word-of-mouth, results, and relentless standards.
